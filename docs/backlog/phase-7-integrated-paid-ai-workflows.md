## Acceptance Criteria

- AI appears as workflow actions, not as a generic top-level assistant.
- Paid/free-credit enforcement applies to AI operations.
- AI-found jobs are deduplicated before import and never auto-saved
  without user review.
- Resume/profile extraction, fit ranking, CV drafts, and application
  material generation are available from relevant workflows.
- AI outputs can be regenerated, reviewed, cached, and saved as tracker
  data.
