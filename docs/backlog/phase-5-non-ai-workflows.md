## Acceptance Criteria

- The app has the final non-AI navigation shape.
- Saved jobs support pipeline, source, contacts, reminders, documents,
  and calendar-related workflows.
- Discover can manage saved searches and imported job candidates without
  AI.
- Duplicate detection protects bulk imports and warns during manual
  create/edit without blocking the user.
